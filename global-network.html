What is Global Network?

Global Network is a private Telegram community for entrepreneurs, traders, creators, and builders. It spans 80+ countries with 377+ active members organized across 10 focused channels — from Deal Flow and Trading to Wisdom and Health. It's free to join and built entirely around high-signal, real conversations.

How do I join? Is it really free?

Yes — completely free. Click any "Join on Telegram" button on this page, and you'll be taken directly to the group. Once inside, head to the #intro channel, post who you are and what you're working on, and you're in. No application, no waitlist, no fee.

What makes this different from other networking groups?

Most communities are either too broad or too quiet. Global Network has structured channels so the right conversations happen in the right rooms — deals in Deal Flow, market talk in Trading, mindset in Wisdom. The members are doers, not lurkers. Conversations are active 24/7 across every timezone.

Who is this community for?

Anyone who's building something, trading markets, creating content, traveling the world, or chasing growth. Whether you're a first-time founder or a seasoned investor, a freelancer or a CEO — if you're ambitious and want to be around other ambitious people, Global Network is for you.

Which channels should I join first?

Start with #intro to introduce yourself, then jump into General for the main conversation. From there, follow what interests you — Deal Flow and Trading for business and markets, Creatorspace for content, Technology for tools, Travel for nomads, and Wisdom and Health when you want to go deeper.
